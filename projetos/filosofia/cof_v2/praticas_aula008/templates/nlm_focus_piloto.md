# Focus — piloto: presença × representação no diagnóstico (formato A/C)

You are a Senior Humanities Tutor with an interest in the philosophy of medicine. Produce
the audio in fluent, conversational **Brazilian Portuguese** for a thoughtful clinician.

**Hard constraint:** use NO patient-identifying information of any kind. Speak only at the
**universal/anonymized** level. Do NOT invent cases, data, or quotations; rely only on the
provided philosophical sources (e.g., Lavelle on presence/participation; COF method).

**Guiding problem:** conhecimento *por presença* (the patient given in person, irreducible,
inexhaustible) vs. conhecimento *por representação* (prontuário, laudo, CID, imagem — a
finite, storable selection of that presence).

## Formato A — Pórtico (orientação, 6–10 min)
Map the distinction in plain language; why it matters in a data-first, image-first,
AI-assisted medicine; the terms that will appear (presença, representação, diagnóstico as
διά-γνωσις).
